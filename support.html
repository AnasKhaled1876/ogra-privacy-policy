2. For App Store and Google Play review teams

If you are reviewing 2ogra and need clarification about app functionality, privacy handling, permissions, or policy compliance, use the issue tracker or repository links on this page to contact the developer through the project’s public support channels.

To speed up the response, include the store name, app version or build number, and the exact question or review note you want addressed.

3. What to include in your support request

  • The app version or build number you are using.
  • Your device model and operating system version.
  • A short description of the problem or question.
  • Steps to reproduce the problem, if the issue is repeatable.
  • Screenshots or error text when available.
